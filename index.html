Barangay Record Management System

During our second year of college, I had the privilege of leading a talented team in the creation of the Barangay Record Management System (BRMS) using Visual Studio and .NET. Our mission was to fashion a user-friendly solution for our local Barangay community, simplifying the task of managing records. A critical achievement in our project was the successful integration of a robust MySQL database, prioritizing data security, accuracy, and easy access for authorized users. With the BRMS, our aim was to empower our community by providing an efficient tool for organizing and handling various records, ultimately contributing to a more organized and efficient Barangay operation.

Tools:

  • VB.NET
  • Visual Studio
  • MySQL
Source Code

Login Page

This is the Login page of the system where you type your Username and Password

Menu page

Menu page, where you can easily manage various aspects of our system. Here, you can handle street information, residents' details, barangay officials' records, clearances, indigency cases, and reports.

Account List Page

This is the Account List or Manage User page of our system, where you can access comprehensive information about employees and accounts created within the system. Additionally, it is important to note that only administrators have the privilege to create new accounts on this page.

Street Page

Welcome to the Street Page. Here, you have the ability to manage and add streets within the system.

Residents Page

Welcome to the Resident's Page. Here, you can easily add new residents to our database by clicking 'New' and inputting their information, which will then be added to the resident list. Additionally, clicking on a specific item in the list will display their detailed information in the Resident Information section, where you also have the capability to print it.

Brgy Officials Page

This is the page where you can enter and manage all the information of our barangay officials.

Brgy Clearance Page

Welcome to this page, where you can issue barangay clearances to specific individuals whose names are listed. To generate a barangay clearance for a resident, simply select their name from the list, and the system will automatically create the clearance for you.

Brgy Indigency Page

Similar to the barangay clearance page, on this page, you can issue barangay indigency certificates to eligible individuals. Just select the resident's name from the list, and the system will generate the indigency certificate automatically.

Complaints Page

In the Complaints Page, you can create a new list of complaints, including the name of the complainant. Additionally, you have the ability to search for respondents with complaints in the resident database, ensuring that individuals have a comprehensive record of their complaints displayed on this page.

Reports Page

In the Reports Page, you have a range of options for generating essential reports. You can print lists of residents, barangay clearances, and indigency certificates issued. Additionally, you can compile and print reports on all recorded complaints. Importantly, you can access demographic data for the barangay community, allowing you to filter residents by street, occupation, and gender, with the flexibility to print customized reports based on your selection.
